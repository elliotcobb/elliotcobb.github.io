The Institute is Tufts University’s premier sketch comedy group. We create and produce original live and video content, shown thrice a semester at assorted venues at Tufts University in Somerville, MA. The Institute was formed in 2006 and since then has been dedicated to making people laugh. This year the Institute’s leadership team includes Production Manager Elaine Beldsoe, Chief Writer Jehan Madhani, and Executive Producer Elliot Charles Cobb. We hold auditions at the start of every fall semester and some spring semesters at the group’s discretion. Stay tuned for news of our next shows by liking our Facebook page. Contact us by emailing tuftsinstitute@gmail.com.
